Blog 17: Limitations of Weather Dashboard

Introduction

Although weather dashboards are useful, they also have certain limitations that affect performance and reliability.

Dependency on Internet

Weather dashboards require a stable internet connection. Without internet access, real-time data cannot be fetched.

API Dependency

The application depends on third-party APIs. If the API service fails or limits requests, the dashboard may not work properly.

Accuracy Issues

Weather data may not always be 100% accurate due to unpredictable environmental changes.

Future Improvements

Offline caching, improved APIs, and AI-based forecasting can help overcome these limitations.
